# 📦 Supply Chain Management with AI Chatbot Web Application Final Year Project

## 🧠 Overview
The Supply Chain Management System with AI Chatbot is a web-based application designed to optimize and automate supply chain operations such as inventory tracking, order management, supplier coordination, and logistics control. This AI-based Integrated Supply Chain Management System with an embedded chatbot is designed to transform the supply chain industry by leveraging artificial intelligence and automation. The system provides a userfriendly and seamless experience tailored to the specific needs of supply chain operations, An integrated **AI Chatbot** enhances user experience by providing instant responses, order status updates, and intelligent recommendations reducing manual effort and improving efficiency across the supply chain.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Automate core supply chain functions like order and inventory management.
- Improve communication between suppliers, distributors, and managers using an AI chatbot.
- Provide real-time visibility and analytics of stock, orders, and suppliers.
- Enhance decision-making and reduce human dependency.

---

## 🚀 Features
- 💬 **AI Chatbot Integration** – Interacts with users, answers queries, and provides real-time updates.  
- 📦 **Inventory Management** – Manage product stocks, reorder levels, and supplier information.  
- 🧾 **Order Management** – Handles order creation, tracking, and delivery status.  
- 🔗 **Supplier & Customer Modules** – Enables communication and transparency between all parties.  
- 📊 **Analytics Dashboard** – Displays real-time statistics and reports for management insights.
